History and Background

Sir Adamjee Institute (SAI) shaped into reality the longing of the Pakistan Memon Educational and Welfare Society (PMEWS) to provide a mass section of society the basic right to get education. PMEWS, constituted on January 26, 1955 was a continuation of All India Memon Educational and Welfare Society formed in 1933.

For PMEWS, the singular purpose of providing high quality education to the deserving and talented sections of society saw new light with its collaboration with the Commecs Educational Trust when it was decided to launch an Intermediate (Commerce) programme in 2004 in the first phase followed by Pre-Engineering in 2005 and Pre-Medical in 2006. It goes to credit of highly qualified and dedicated teaching staff that the college achieved 100% result in its first appearance at BIE Annual Examinations. The college, since then has maintained its reputation and has emerged as one of the premier institution of Karachi. SAI is an everlasting tribute and acknowledgment to a great visionary of the sub- continent, Sir Adamjee Haji Dawood.

Choose your field of Study

Commerce

The college was started with Commerce group in 2004. Accounting, Banking, Principles of Commerce, Commercial Geography, Business Studies, Economics, and Statistics are taught using a practical approach to make learning engaging and approachable of the students choosing commerce faculty.

Pre-Engineering

The Pre-Engineering group was started in 2005 under the direction of a science faculty that was devoted, experienced, and highly motivated. The different fields of engineering such as Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Aeronautical, Architectural, Telecom, Chemical, Space & others.

Pre-Medical

The Pre-Medical group was introduced in the year 2006. It serves as a gateway to a variety of medical professions, including physiotherapy, surgery, pharmacy, microbiology, molecular genetics, biochemistry, biotechnology, and bioinformatics, among others.

Computer Science

The Computer Science group was introduced in 2019. The programme develops the basic scientific skills of the students in the fields of Physics, Computer Science and Mathematics through lectures and practicals (programing language etc).

Salient Features

Highly Qualified And Experienced Faculty

The highly qualified and experienced faculty is the greatest asset of the SAI. Without any shadow of doubt, it is the members of the teaching community who impart progress into the students. Drawn from the reputed universities of Pakistan. SAI faculty believes in character building.

Co/Extra curricular activities

Extra-curricular activities alongwith academic activities help students to learn about themselves and develop and use their skills and knowledge in different contexts. Such activities are an essential element of the college experience.

Performance based merit scholarships

We offer merit scholarships to the meritorious students, on their continuous academic performance in Mid-Term and Preliminary Examinations. Which creates competitive environment with financial relief and increase their confidence.

Well stocked Library

The importance of a well stocked library within a college has a greater impact that one could possibly imagine. The library, like a wise old owl perched on a tree branch would be the place that everyone would come for free advice, guidance or peace.

Fee endowment

SAI believes that everyone has the equal right to get quality education. This is the sole reason behind our Endowment programme. The students who cannot afford to pay the college fee are given endowments to keep their education continuing. SAI has so far bestowed endowments to many deserving students.

Modern Science Labs

In order to cater the needs of the students, Physics, Chemistry and Biology Laboratories were set up. The laboratories are fully equipped with all modern scientific facilities and apparatus for students to do their experiments, practical assignments.

Generally Asked Questions

When does admission start in Intermediate?

Admission process normally begins in April and continues till July. New session starts from August..

Is there any age relaxation (just a few days) possible?

Applicants must not be above 18 years of age on August 1st, in the year of application. However, those more than 18 years of age but less than 19 can be admitted subject to approval of the Chairman-SAI.

Relaxation up to 1 year is given to Huffaz-e-Quran (candidates are required to show their “Sanad”)

What sports facilities are available at College?

We have in campus facilities for Table Tennis, Badminton, Basketball and Throw Ball. We participate in Cricket, Badminton, Throw Ball, Football, Hockey, Tennis and Table Tennis tournaments.

Are there any opportunities for academic creativity (written and spoken) at the College?

There are a wide range of co-curricular activities which enhance a student’s written and spoken skills. SAI publications include Gul-e-SAI, students are always encouraged to participate. There are in-house and intercollegiate competitions like Bilingual Declamations and Writing Competitions. Our students also participate in inter-collegiate competitions. Each year students stage skits as well as English & Urdu stage shows.
